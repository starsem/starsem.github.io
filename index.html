THE JOINT CONFERENCE ON LEXICAL and COMPUTATIONAL SEMANTICS

*SEM is a series of conferences that bring together researchers interested in the semantics of natural languages and its computational modeling. The conference embraces symbolic and probabilistic approaches, and everything in between; theoretical contributions as well as practical applications are welcome. The long-term goal of *SEM is to provide a stable forum for the growing number of NLP researchers working on all aspects of semantics.
